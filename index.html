Our Story

Your little escape
from the rush

Hovv Patio and Farms was born from a simple idea — that everyone deserves a place to slow down. Tucked away from the chaos of the city, we built a space where wooden tables, warm lights, and the smell of freshly brewed coffee greet you at the door.

We source fresh ingredients, brew with care, and plate with love. Whether you're catching up with an old friend, on a quiet first date, pulling an all-nighter, or just treating yourself — this is your place.
